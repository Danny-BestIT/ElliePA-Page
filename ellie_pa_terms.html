2. Service Description

Ellie PA connects to WhatsApp groups by being added as a participant by the group’s administrators. The bot has no access rights beyond those of a regular group member: it can read messages, send messages, and view participants and admins.

Ellie PA processes this data to:

  • Generate daily summaries
  • Respond to user queries
  • Provide contextual insights based on past conversations

Data is transmitted to third-party services for processing:

  • OpenAI - for text analysis and summarization
  • Supabase - for database storage (Frankfurt, Zurich, or Stockholm)
